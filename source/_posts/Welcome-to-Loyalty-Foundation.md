We are delighted to welcome you to the Loyalty Foundation website!

## Our Mission: Love • Infinite (愛•無窮)

The Loyalty Foundation is dedicated to promoting a culture of care throughout Hong Kong. We believe that:

- **[Poor 貧]** means a lack of resources
- **[Poverty 窮]** means there is no way out

<!-- more -->

Our foundation works tirelessly to provide resources with love and help find ways out together. We aim to pass on the belief of **[Love•Infinite]** to every community in Hong Kong.

## Supporting Post-Pandemic Recovery

We are committed to helping low-income families facing post-pandemic recovery challenges. Through our various programs, members can:

- Support each other through difficult times
- Achieve self-reliance and independence
- Work together to overcome obstacles

## Get Involved

There are many ways you can support our mission:

1. **Donate**: Your contributions help us provide essential resources to those in need
2. **Volunteer**: Join our team and make a direct impact in the community
3. **Spread the Word**: Share our message and help us reach more people

Together, we can build a stronger, more caring community.
